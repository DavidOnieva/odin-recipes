Quiche recipe

Quiche Lorraine is a timeless classic. This version has bacon, Swiss cheese, and onions in perfect harmony amidst eggs and cream for a supremely satisfying brunch dish!

This quiche Lorraine has a rich and creamy bacon and egg filling. It is the perfect crowd-pleasing addition to Easter brunch.

Quiche Lorraine is a French tart made with eggs, cream, pork, and sometimes cheese. The dish is native to Lorraine, a region in Northeastern France. It can be served warm or cold.

    ingredients
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Pie crust
  • Cream
  • Eggs
  • Salt and sugar
  • Cayenne
    step-by-step
  1. Cook, chop, and drain the bacon.
  2. Sprinkle the bacon, cheese, and onion in the shell.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients and seasonings, then pour the mixture into the shell.
  4. Bake until a knife comes out clean
