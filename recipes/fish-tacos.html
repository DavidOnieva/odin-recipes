Lasagna recipe

These crispy fish tacos with shredded cabbage and a spicy homemade citrusy sauce are just delicious! Serve with pico de gallo and lime wedges to squeeze on top.

You don’t have to leave your house to enjoy delicious fish tacos! This recipe makes perfectly seasoned, beer-battered crispy fish for tacos and tops them with a homemade zesty sauce and shredded cabbage.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

    Ingredients:
  • Homemade white sauce (made with yogurt, mayonnaise, lime juice, jalapeños, capers, cayenne pepper, oregano, cumin, and dill weed) and fresh cabbage
  • Sliced avocados
  • Onions
  • Cotija cheese
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Cod filets
    Step-by-step:
  1. Make the beer batter.
  2. Make the white sauce.
  3. Dust the fish in flour, then dip in the batter.
  4. Fry the fish until golden brown.
  5. Serve on tortillas with the sauce and cabbage.
